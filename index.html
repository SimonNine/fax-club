The Experiment

A real, quiet revolution.

For one year, 100 strangers received one question per week. They thought deeply. They wrote honestly. They stayed completely anonymous. No likes. No followers. No algorithms. Just raw, human thinking on paper.

The Fax Club Experiment captures the most powerful moments from 52 weeks of radical curiosity — distilled into a book that feels like eavesdropping on the most honest conversation you've never been invited to.
